About Relay615

A boutique integration consultancy with deep roots in enterprise middleware, EDI, healthcare data, and cloud migration.

Our Story

Built Around One Discipline

Relay615 was founded on a simple premise: integration consulting is better when it’s all you do. We’ve seen what happens when large generalist firms send junior consultants to tackle BizTalk migrations or HL7 implementations — projects run over budget, timelines slip, and business logic gets lost in translation.

Our answer was to build a firm where every consultant is a specialist, every engagement has senior involvement from day one, and every client gets the kind of attention you’d only expect from a trusted partner.

Headquartered in Tennessee, we serve clients across North America — from regional health systems to Fortune 500 supply chains.

What Guides Us

Our Values

These aren’t wall posters. They’re the principles that shape how we scope work, communicate with clients, and make decisions when things get difficult.

Honest Assessment

We’ll tell you if something isn’t worth migrating or if a simpler approach exists. Our long-term reputation matters more than a larger statement of work.

Senior Accountability

The architects who sign off on a design are the same people who implement it. Accountability doesn’t get diluted through layers of management.

Depth Over Breadth

We say no to projects outside our specialty. That discipline keeps our skills sharp and our clients confident they’re getting the best available expertise.

Delivery Discipline

Agile doesn’t mean vague. We define scope clearly, estimate carefully, and communicate early when anything changes. On-time and on-budget is the expectation, not the exception.
